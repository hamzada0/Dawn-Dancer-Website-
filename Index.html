About

Dawn Dancer is dedicated to providing support, education, and resources for those developing or teaching independent living skills. We believe everyone deserves the opportunity to live independently and thrive.

Programs

  • Daily Living Skills: Cooking, cleaning, hygiene, and home maintenance.
  • Financial Literacy: Budgeting, saving, and understanding bills.
  • Employment Readiness: Job applications, interviews, and workplace behavior.
  • Health & Wellness: Nutrition, exercise, and mental health awareness.
